About Me

My Story

Hello!

Hi, I’m Fiona. I’m a UX/UI designer and artist with a knowledge of frontend development. I'm based in Edinburgh, UK and am currently available for projects and full time roles in central Scotland and remotely.

Journey into UX

I’m curious about design psychology and enjoy creatively solving problems. I love to create memorable, engaging experiences for people through my designs. I’ve journeyed into UX via a background in jewellery design followed by over ten years in retail display. Alongside, I’ve worked on personal projects like writing and illustrating a children’s book. On the way, I’ve honed a mix of empathy, imagination and strong attention to detail. Whether it’s for the wearer, customer, viewer or reader, my goal is to create designs that work on all levels, from practical to tangible to emotional.

Moving into UX, I would love to bring my skills to collaborate on innovative projects that can have a positive impact on people’s lives and the world around us.

Interests

I always enjoy learning new things and am currently discovering the world of HTML and CSS. This website is my first coding project!

In my free time I enjoy travel and the outdoors. I love exploring new places as well as wandering like a tourist in my home city!
